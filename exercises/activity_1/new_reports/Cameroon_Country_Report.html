1 Life expectancy in Cameroon

The average life expectancy in Cameroon between 1980 and 2010 was 52.46. The minimum life expectancy in Cameroon between 1980 and 2010 was 49.86. The maximum life expectancy in Cameroon between 1980 and 2010 was 54.98.
